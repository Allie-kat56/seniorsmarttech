Common Questions

Which smartwatch has the best fall detection for seniors?

The Apple Watch Series 10 and Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 are the gold standards. They feature sophisticated algorithms that differentiate between a heavy trip and a dangerous fall, providing an essential safety net.

Can a smartwatch monitor bone density?

While wrist wearables cannot yet scan bone, they are the perfect hub for that data. By using a Vitality Station (a BIA-enabled smart scale), you can monitor bone mineral content and muscle mass trends daily on your watch face.
